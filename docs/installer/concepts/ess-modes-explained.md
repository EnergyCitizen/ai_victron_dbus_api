## ESS Modes (Brief)

- **Optimized (with BatteryLife)**: Default, extends battery life
- **Optimized (without BatteryLife)**: Maximum self-consumption
- **Keep batteries charged**: Grid backup priority
- **External control**: MQTT or Modbus control

## Why It Matters

Choosing the wrong ESS mode can:
- Shorten battery life
- Increase electricity costs
- Cause system instability
- Reduce backup time
